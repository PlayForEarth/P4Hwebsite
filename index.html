The Measurement Standard

HAI: we measure what actually happens — not what patients say.

The Health Adherence Index tells you whether care behavior is genuinely embedded — or just happening under programme pressure.

HAI = (F × 0.30) + (P × 0.25) + (C × 0.20)
+ (E × 0.15) + (R × 0.10)
